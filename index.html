Our Story

Jusam Medical Limited was born from a dream — the dream of Samuel Tuva, a young boy once rescued by Faraja Children's Home, who grew up to become a nurse, founder, and healthcare leader.

Through the support of groups like NSP-Aid, Samuel overcame hardship and dedicated his life to giving back. Jusam Medical Clinic is now his way of passing that care forward — one patient at a time.

"Jusam is not just a clinic — it's a place of hope, healing, and human dignity."
